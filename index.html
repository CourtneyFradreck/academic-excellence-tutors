Pricing

We provide high-quality online tutoring services at reasonable prices. Our monthly fees vary depending on the number of subjects you choose to enroll in. The fee structure is as follows:
  • One subject: R70
  • Two subjects: R130
  • Three subjects: R180
  • Four subjects: R250
These fees apply to students who are currently in school. For students who wish to rewrite a metric subject, there is a one-time registration fee of R50 in addition to the monthly fee of R150. Therefore, the total fee for the first month is R200, and R150 for the subsequent months.
